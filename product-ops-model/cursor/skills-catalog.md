# Cursor Skills Catalog for Product Teams

Use these skills to standardize how teams use AI in product work.

## Skill 1: Opportunity Framing Assistant

### Purpose
Turn raw notes into a clear opportunity statement with evidence and measurable outcomes.

### Inputs
- Interview notes/transcripts.
- Product metrics snapshot.
- Strategy context.

### Output
- Draft opportunity assessment (using template).
- Top assumptions with confidence levels.

### Quality checks
- Is every claim traceable to evidence?
- Are outcomes measurable?
- Are assumptions explicit?

## Skill 2: Discovery Synthesizer

### Purpose
Cluster qualitative and quantitative signals into validated insight candidates.

### Inputs
- Discovery evidence log.
- Interview summaries.
- Support/sales themes.

### Output
- Insight clusters with confidence tags.
- Contradictions and unresolved questions.

### Quality checks
- Avoid fabricated certainty.
- Keep facts separate from interpretation.

## Skill 3: Experiment Designer

### Purpose
Generate test options for key assumptions with success criteria and risks.

### Inputs
- Assumption statement.
- User segment.
- Constraints (time, tooling, risk).

### Output
- Experiment card draft.
- Instrumentation and measurement suggestions.

### Quality checks
- Includes falsifiable criteria.
- Includes guardrail metrics.
- Identifies ethical/privacy risks.

## Skill 4: PRD-Lite Co-pilot

### Purpose
Draft a concise implementation brief from opportunity and discovery inputs.

### Inputs
- Opportunity assessment.
- Discovery brief.
- Technical constraints.

### Output
- PRD-lite draft with scope, requirements, and rollout approach.

### Quality checks
- Scope is outcome-linked.
- Out-of-scope is explicit.
- Risks and dependencies are clear.

## Skill 5: Post-Launch Learning Analyst

### Purpose
Summarize outcomes vs expectations and recommend next decisions.

### Inputs
- KPI dashboard outputs.
- Qualitative feedback.
- Incident or support data.

### Output
- Learning summary: continue, iterate, pause, or stop.

### Quality checks
- Distinguishes signal from noise.
- Explains uncertainty and confidence.

## Suggested team practice

- Assign one "AI artifact owner" per initiative.
- Pair AI output generation with human review by PM + one cross-functional partner.
- Keep a prompt and output library for reuse and quality improvement.
