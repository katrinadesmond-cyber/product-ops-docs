# 04. Implementation Roadmap (30-60-90 Days)

## Goal

Roll out the AI Product Operations model in a practical, low-friction sequence that builds adoption and measurable value.

## Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Foundation and alignment

### Outcomes
- Leadership and team alignment on operating principles.
- Core templates and standards published.
- Pilot teams identified.

### Actions
1. Run kickoff workshop on framework and expected behaviors.
2. Baseline current state:
   - discovery practices;
   - artifact quality;
   - decision cadence;
   - AI usage patterns.
3. Publish standards and templates.
4. Enable Cursor rules and team prompt playbooks.
5. Select 2-3 pilot initiatives.

### Exit criteria
- Standards accepted.
- Pilot teams staffed and onboarded.
- Baseline metrics captured.

## Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Pilot and iterate

### Outcomes
- Pilots execute using full artifact stack.
- Early proof points and gaps identified.

### Actions
1. Coach pilots weekly.
2. Run discovery cadence with quality checkpoints.
3. Track adoption and cycle metrics.
4. Collect pain points on templates and rules.
5. Revise framework based on pilot feedback.

### Exit criteria
- Pilot teams demonstrate evidence-based decisions.
- At least one measurable improvement (speed, clarity, or impact).
- Updated v2 templates/rules published.

## Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Scale and institutionalize

### Outcomes
- Framework adopted across product teams.
- Governance and reporting operating consistently.

### Actions
1. Expand enablement to all teams.
2. Start monthly insights publication.
3. Run quarterly health review cadence.
4. Embed framework onboarding into new team member ramp-up.
5. Define ownership model for ongoing framework maintenance.

### Exit criteria
- Majority of active initiatives use standard artifacts.
- Discovery and learning cadence sustained.
- Product Ops governance routine running without heroics.

## Maturity model

### Level 1: Emerging
- Inconsistent artifact quality.
- Discovery activity varies by team.
- AI usage mostly ad hoc.

### Level 2: Defined
- Shared templates and minimum standards in place.
- Discovery cadence running in most teams.
- Rules for responsible AI use established.

### Level 3: Scaled
- Portfolio-level insights and benchmarks available.
- Teams reliably make evidence-backed decisions.
- AI assists are integrated safely into core workflows.

### Level 4: Adaptive
- Framework continuously improved from operational feedback.
- Discovery and delivery loops are tightly connected.
- Strategic prioritization is dynamic and evidence-led.

## Change management checklist

- [ ] Executive sponsor confirmed.
- [ ] Product Ops owner assigned.
- [ ] Pilot teams selected.
- [ ] Training sessions scheduled.
- [ ] Templates/socialization plan published.
- [ ] Review cadence on calendars.
- [ ] Success metrics dashboard live.
